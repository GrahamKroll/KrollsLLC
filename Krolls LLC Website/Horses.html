  • $350 - stable provides hay and feeds twice a day and owner mucks every 1-4 days
  • $425 - stable provides hay and feeds twice a day and mucks
  • $250 Deposit needed to hold stall. You will get the deposit back when you leave if there are no damages. Negative Coggins test and health cert needed to move your horse in.
